Depending on context, testing objectives may include:

- Verifying requirements are met
- Validating system fulfills user needs
- Building confidence in quality level
- Detecting failures and defects
- Preventing defects
- Providing information to stakeholders
- Reducing risk
- Complying with contractual/legal requirements

🔎 Real-World Example (Banking App)

Requirement: “User can transfer funds up to daily limit.”
    Verification → Check system enforces limit.
    Validation → Confirm limit aligns with regulatory rules.
    Risk reduction → Prevent over-transfer fraud.
